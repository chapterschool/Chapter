Admission Information

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds. Admission is based on merit and available spaces in each class.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Pick up an admission form at the school office or download it online.
  2. Submit the completed form with all required documents.
  3. Attend the entrance examination and interview.
  4. Successful applicants will receive admission letters.

For more details, contact the admissions office at info@luisantschool.com.
